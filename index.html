Katrina and her thoughts.

Imperfections don't make anyone perfect, but make you the person you are. Some imperfections are harmful, and DO need to be changed, but that is up to your personal judgement and decision. It is in human nature to search for the things one does not have, in the end, one fails to realize what they had from the beginning. Self love begins when you begin to see the small things you have never appreciated in yourself. It begins when you see the things you have accomplished, no matter how large or small they may be. The next time you feel terrible about yourself, remember that the one thing you're focusing on, does not define who you are. Self love won't take a few hours or days, this can take years, but you can do this and you are never alone. There will be others to pick you up and be right beside you when you fall, but at the end of the day, it's up to you to choose how you see yourself. At the end of the day, whether or not you realize it; nothing in the world ever was and never will be equal to the worth of your life and the wonderful things you do for the world.
